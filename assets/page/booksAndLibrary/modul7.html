Module 7: Agreement

A. Objectives

After studying this material, students are able to:

  • Identify expressions of agreement or disagreement in short conversations

B. Material Description

Agreement is the act of expressing harmony or consent with what another person says.

Common Agreement Phrases:

  • "So do I", "Neither do I", "I agree", "That’s right", "Exactly"
  • For negative agreement: “Neither do I”, “I don’t either”
  • Disagreement: “I don’t think so”, “Not really”, “Actually, no”

Example

Man: I think the test was very hard.
Woman: I agree. I could hardly answer any questions.
Narrator: What does the woman mean?

Answer: A. She also found the test difficult.

Exercise 7.1

Underline expressions of agreement or disagreement and choose the correct interpretation of the speaker’s opinion.

C. Review Exercises

Identify whether each response is in agreement, disagreement, or expressing uncertainty.
