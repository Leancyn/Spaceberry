Module 8: Suggestions & Uncertainty

A. Objectives

After studying this material, students are able to:

  • Understand how suggestions and uncertainty are expressed in English conversation

B. Material Description

Suggestions:

  • "Why don’t you...?", "Let’s...", "You could...", "I suggest..."

Uncertainty expressions:

  • "I’m not sure", "I guess", "Probably", "Maybe", "It seems that..."

Example

Woman: What should I wear to the interview?
Man: Maybe you should wear something formal.
Narrator: What does the man mean?

Answer: B. He’s suggesting a formal outfit.

Exercise 8.1

Underline any suggestion or expression of uncertainty and analyze its meaning.

C. Review Exercises

Distinguish between clear suggestions and indirect uncertainty in various responses.
