Module 9: Review Exercises

A. Objectives

  • Recognize idiomatic expressions in short conversations
  • Understand the meaning of idioms based on context

B. Material Description

Idioms are phrases whose meanings are not literal. For example:

  • “Hit the books” means to study hard
  • “Break the ice” means to start a conversation in a social setting
  • “Under the weather” means feeling sick

Understanding idioms is crucial to comprehend informal and spoken English effectively.

C. Review Exercises

Identify idiomatic expressions in short conversations and interpret their meanings correctly.
